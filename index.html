Welcome to "Gentle care" Mobile Dentistry

"Gentle care" Mobile Dentistry provides fully functional mobile dental care and oral hygiene services to elderly residing in long term care homes, throughout Vancouver, Burnaby, Port Coquitlam, Surrey, Hope.

Our dental team consists of Dentist and Dental hygienist & dental assistants. When we prepare any denture we make sure that fittings are of a high quality.

Our dental procedures are focused on maximizing functionality and minimizing discomfort, and avoiding medical emergencies


  • Your oral health can effect your overall health. ensuring you have good oral hygiene can reduce your risk of heart disease and stroke
  • Make sure you floss everyday to stop plaque building up in between your teeth and around the gumline.
  • Brushing twice a day with a flouride toothpaste will help to ensure that your teeth are in their best health, and can help prevent decay
  • Keep your dentures in good condition by cleaning them thouroughly everyday
