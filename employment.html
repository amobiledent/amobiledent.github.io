Working with us

Benefits of working with "Gentle Care"

We aim to provide a working environment that fosters learning and development, health and well being, fair treatment and the ability to balance work, life and family commitments. We are an equal opportunity employer, not just in word but in deed!

Positions Vacant

If you are a Dentist who would like to work a day or two through the week, away from your current environment, give us a call on 604-614-5078 and we might just be able to help!

How can I apply?

For most positions vacant we will have it listed with Seek or you can simply follow the listed link under the Positions Vacant section on this page to find out more about the job.

All prospective applicants are encouraged to speak with the contact person to clarify what the position requires before continuing with the registration requirements. This is also a good time to ask for a position description to be forwarded to you.

Please Note

Should you decide to apply for a position at "Gentle Care" we require the following;

  • a covering letter that includes the position being applied for,
  • a current Resume / CV,
  • at least two (2) recent referees who have been in a senior position and have worked with you.

Should you be asked to come in for an interview, you will need to bring with you original documentation in relation to your;

  • qualifications,
  • current Registrations,
  • current Police name check (if held),
  • current Working with Children's check(if held).
