School Dentistry Solutions for lowermainland

At "Gentle care" Mobile Dentistry we believe that good oral hygiene starts at an early age, we also recognise that it can sometimes be hard for parents to organise trips to the dentist around work and other commitments which is why our fully equipped mobile dental clinics provide an easily accessible solution for school age children to access vital dental services.

Our Mobile Dental clinics can visit your school and provide dental treatment to students eligible under the Healthy Kids programme and if a student is ineligible for the Healthy Kids service we are still able to treat them with the charge of a private fee. You can find out more about the Healthy Kids here

This service is provided at no charge to your school making it a cost effective strategy for managing your student's oral health.

Why use "Gentle care" Mobile Dentistry for your school?

  • All of our staff are fully qualified, carry a police check and hold a working with children check
  • No cost to the school for providing dental treatment
